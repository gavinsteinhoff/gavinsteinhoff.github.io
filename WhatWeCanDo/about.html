What We Can Do

What We Can Do is about showing what can be accomplished when people work together

What We Can Do aims to provide volunteer opportunities to students all year long

The first Saturday of every month, we go out and serve the community at various organizations across Topeka

Contest

What We Can Do encourages participation by rewarding whichever student organization had the most participation throughout the year

The reward is $500 towards the student organization and $500 towards a charity of their choice
